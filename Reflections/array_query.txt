1. LOOKUP TABLE
   a. Space complexity: O(n^2)
   b. Time Complexity:O(n^2)
   c. Query time: O(1)

2. SQUARE ROOT DECOMPOSITION
   a. Space Complexity: O(sqrt(n))
   b. Time Complexity: O(n)
   c. Divided into blocks of fixed range

3. SEGMENT TREES
   a. Space Complexity: O(4n)
   b. Time Complexity:  O(log(n))
   c. Use it when the data is static
